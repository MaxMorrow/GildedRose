Welcome

Good evening, and welcome to the Gilded Rose. There are a few things you should know before you get started managing our fine inventory.

We're starting out with a standard set of items, so you'll only be able to purchase whole inventories of our standard grouping at the moment. To do so, provide the name of the inventory and the owner, then click 'Purchase Inventory' below and we'll have you all set up.

After that, you'll be stocked and ready to manage your first inventory in our shop. You can do so by clicking 'Inventories' to the left.

To open an inventory, click on a chest and you'll be able to view your items.
